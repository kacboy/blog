8BitDo Micro: Compact Bluetooth Gaming Controller

The 8BitDo Micro Bluetooth Controller is a compact and versatile gaming accessory that's making waves in the portable gaming world. Priced at around $25 USD or $35 CAD, this pint-sized powerhouse offers a range of features that make it an attractive option for gamers on the go. Let's take a closer look at what this tiny controller has to offer.

Unboxing and Design: A Pocket-Sized Gaming Marvel

Upon unboxing the 8BitDo Micro Bluetooth Controller, you'll find the controller itself, a color-coordinated wrist strap, a short USB-C to USB-A cable, and an instruction manual. The controller comes in two eye-catching colors: green and blue.

Despite its small size, the controller packs a punch with its feature set. It includes all the essential buttons you'd expect:

  • D-pad
  • ABXY buttons (in Nintendo layout)
  • L1, R1, L2, and R2 buttons
  • Plus and minus buttons
  • Star button
  • 8BitDo home button

The buttons have a satisfying click to them, providing good tactile feedback. One notable omission is the lack of analog sticks, which limits its compatibility with some modern games but makes it perfect for retro and arcade-style titles.

Functionality and Compatibility: A Versatile Portable Bluetooth Gamepad

The 8BitDo Micro Bluetooth Controller offers impressive functionality for its size. On the bottom of the device, you'll find a switch with SDK options and a sync button. The SDK options include:

  • Switch mode for Nintendo Switch compatibility
  • Keyboard mode for programmable Bluetooth functionality
  • D-input mode for general gamepad use

This versatility allows the controller to work with a wide range of devices and platforms. However, it's worth noting that the lack of analog sticks may limit its use in some modern games that require precise 360-degree movement.

8BitDo Ultimate Software App: Unleash Your Controller's Potential

One of the standout features of this controller is its compatibility with the 8BitDo Ultimate Software app. This app allows you to customize the controller's buttons and create multiple profiles for different use cases. Here's how to get started:

  1. Download the 8BitDo Ultimate Software app
  2. Turn on the controller and set it to pairing mode
  3. Connect the controller to your device via Bluetooth
  4. Set the controller to keyboard mode
  5. Open the app and start customizing

The app lets you assign any button on the controller to various keyboard functions, making it an incredibly versatile tool. Some unique use cases include:

  • Controlling e-readers to flip pages remotely
  • Operating teleprompters for video production
  • Using it as a remote selfie button for your smartphone

Gaming Performance: A Retro Gaming Controller's Dream

While the 8BitDo Micro Bluetooth Controller may not be suitable for all modern games, it shines when it comes to retro and arcade-style gaming. Its compact size and responsive buttons make it perfect for classic platformers, fighting games, and other titles that don't require analog input.

Interestingly, you can even use this controller with PlayStation Remote Play through third-party apps like PX Play. While it won't work with the official PlayStation Remote Play app (which only supports DualShock 4, DualSense, or Backbone devices), third-party solutions open up new possibilities for PlayStation gamers on the go.

One minor inconvenience is the Nintendo button layout, which swaps the positions of some buttons compared to the PlayStation layout. However, this can be easily remedied through in-game controls, system settings, or the remote play app itself.

Pros and Cons: Weighing the 8BitDo Controller Review

Pros:

  • Extremely portable and travel-friendly
  • Versatile with multiple connectivity options
  • Customizable through the 8BitDo Ultimate Software app
  • Affordable price point
  • Great for retro and arcade-style games

Cons:

  • Lack of analog sticks limits compatibility with some modern games
  • Small size may not be comfortable for extended gaming sessions
  • Nintendo button layout may require adjustment for PlayStation users

In the end, the 8BitDo Micro Bluetooth Controller offers impressive value for its price. Its portability, versatility, and customization options make it an attractive option for gamers who prioritize on-the-go gaming or those looking for a compact, multipurpose Bluetooth remote.

While it may not replace your primary gaming controller, it's an excellent complement to your gaming arsenal, especially for retro gaming enthusiasts or those who need a programmable Bluetooth remote for various devices.

FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

What devices is the 8BitDo Micro Bluetooth Controller compatible with?

The controller is compatible with a wide range of devices, including Nintendo Switch, PCs, smartphones, and tablets. It can also be used with PlayStation Remote Play through third-party apps.

Can I use the 8BitDo Micro Bluetooth Controller for modern console games?

While it can be used for some modern games, the lack of analog sticks limits its compatibility with many contemporary titles that require precise 360-degree movement.

How long does the battery last on the 8BitDo Micro Bluetooth Controller?

The battery life varies depending on usage, but it typically lasts for several hours of gameplay. It can be recharged using the included USB-C cable.

Is the 8BitDo Ultimate Software app available for both iOS and Android?

Yes, the 8BitDo Ultimate Software app is available for both iOS and Android devices, allowing users on both platforms to customize their controller.

Can I use the 8BitDo Micro Bluetooth Controller with emulators?

Yes, the controller works well with many emulators, making it an excellent choice for retro gaming on various devices.
